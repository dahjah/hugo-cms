★★★★ ★

First things first, the ranch is amazing. It's a black peppercorn ranch and just phenomenal. Fries are great too, they are the hand-cut fries that are personally my favorite! They literally prepare all the food as your order - so nothing is frozen or cooked beforehand. Which to me, means it's made with love. Onto the chicken, the flavor is 100% on point. It's delicious, you can tell the chicken is of quality and it's a good tender. I do find the breading interesting as it's extremely thin and not necessarily crispy. So I would say the breading is more like a coating and it reminds me of grilled chicken tenders with a bit of extra texture. All in all, the flavors are on point and and aren't your usual chicken tender flavor - plus the fact that it's all made to order is really awesome! Looking forward to trying this place again.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance for most events. For weddings and large corporate events, 1-2 months is ideal to ensure availability.
What is your service area?
We primarily serve the Salt Lake City metro area and surrounding Utah counties. Contact us for events outside this area - we may still be able to accommodate!
Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?
Absolutely! We offer vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. Let us know your guests' needs and we'll customize the menu accordingly.
What's included in your catering packages?
Our packages include food preparation, serving staff, all necessary equipment, setup, and cleanup. We arrive fully self-sufficient - you just need to provide the space!
How much does it cost?
Pricing varies based on guest count, menu selection, and event type. Most events range from $15-35 per person. Contact us for a custom quote!

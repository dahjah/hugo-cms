Find Us Today

Welcome to Strippin Dippin Chicken

Jerremy and Ashley started their food truck journey in fall 2021. Fresh chicken tenders and fresh-cut fries (cut right on the truck) with all house-made sauces! After owning a fencing business for 5 years, we decided to pursue Jerremy's dream of serving great food.

We developed our chicken and fries concept to ensure the freshest chicken tenders and fresh-cut potatoes - cut right on the truck for each order. All our sauces are Jerremy's own recipes, including our famous black peppercorn ranch!

Why Choose Us

Fresh & Made to Order

We prepare every order fresh - cutting potatoes and battering chicken right on the truck.

Hand-Cut Fries

Our fries are cut fresh from whole potatoes for each order, perfectly salted and crispy.

House-Made Sauces

All our sauces are Jerremy's own recipes, including our famous black peppercorn ranch.
